This server runs most (but not all) subdomains of Scheme.org.

Managed by Ansible via https://github.com/schemeorg/linux-configurations

Docker can be operated by all users who are in the `docker` group.

Do not keep private information here. Everything is group readable.

Production sites (*.scheme.org) are in /production/*

Staging sites (*.staging.scheme.org) are in /staging/*
